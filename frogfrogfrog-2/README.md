# Frogfrogfrog
A simple, fun, and timing-based mini game made with **p5.js**.

[Play it!](https://pippinbarr.github.io/cart253-examples/topics/making/frogfrogfrog/index.html)

The player controls a frog at the bottom of the screen.  
Click to shoot the frog’s tongue and try to eat as many flies as possible before the timer runs out.

## Game Rules
- when the game start, have ten sconed count down.
- when eat the black fly +1 mark
- Every third fly is yellow , when player eat that fly + 3 mark.
- Target: before the time over eat 8 flies 
- Time return to zero, didn't eat 8 flies, the game over.
- Eat 8 flies, you win!

## How to Play
- Move your mouse to move the frog left and right.
- Click to shoot the frog’s tongue.
- Try to catch the flies before time runs out.

## Game Features
-  The frog has a **blinking eye animation**
- The flies have **flapping wings** and move in a **wave-like up-and-down motion**
- **Yellow flies** introduce time-based tension and pacing changes
- The background includes a **sky, clouds, green lake, and lily pads**
- The end screen features a **rotating frog animation**
